About Us

Cahaya Riset Teknologi is the brainchild of Christian Gabriel. As an ICT consultant with extensive years of experience in the field, he has been successfully assisting as well as solving ICT problems for many different clients, ranging from private to the government sector.


During his years as consultant, Christian often witnessed his clients frustration when they were facing ICT issues. Many of his Clients confessed that the existing solutions in the market frequentll failed to fulfill their wants, needs, and expectations. Most of solutions came with very limited options, mediocre quality, unreliable performance, inflexible offers, and veryoften the preferref solutions did not match their allocated budget.


Concerned by this common problems, Christian decided to change that and established Cahaya Riset Teknologi (CRT). He knows that he has the right knowledge, skills, and experience that can help and assist clients removing all those frustractions when handling ICT issues.


Designing realiable integrated ICT configuration is one of Christian expertise. He immensely enjoy proposing reliable, innovative, and creative ICT solution that matches client budget. His one and only aim is long-term client satisfaction. It has been alwalys the primary focus of his business activities. It is the drive and reason that motivates and inspired him to establish the company. It is the basis of Cahaya Riset Teknologi values and culture.
